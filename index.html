About

I am a graduate student in Applied Linguistics at Teachers College, Columbia University. My research focuses on language assessment, automated writing evaluation (AWE), AI-mediated feedback, and corpus-informed pedagogy. I work with both experimental and corpus-based approaches to understand how technology can enhance L2 writing instruction, assessment validity, and learner development.

Research Interests

  • Automated Essay Scoring (AES) & construct validity
  • AI-mediated feedback for L2 writing
  • Corpus linguistics in language assessment
  • Teacher roles in technology-enhanced writing instruction
